My Favorite photographers

Sebastião Salgado

Brazilian documentary photographer. He traveled in 120 countries for his projects, and made exhibitions throughout the world. His works are all taking from a film Leica camera, meaning NO PHOTOSHOP or filer. You see a photographer's talent in its purety.

Helmut Newton

German-Australian fashion photographer. He pioneered "provocative" fashion photography, and still is the source of inspiration for many commercial photographers. He is a master in black and white photography, and genius in composition. See it yourself!

Daidō Moriyama

Japan pioneered abstract photography in the 60s (totally messy, maybe blurry, but cool photos). Other than Araki, Moriyama is the most notable photographer of this genre, noted for his depiction the breakdown of traditional values in post-war Japan. Google him, and you'll see countrless great photos that will fit well as Grunge album covers.
